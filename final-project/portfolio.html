Graphic design 2

I just finished up a project for graphic design, where we were asked to create a retail box for a product of our choice. I chose to redesign a shoebox for nike, specifically one for Kyrie Irving.

The box is now fully completed and colored from top to bottom.


Picture

Please keep in mind that the box was designed with professionalism and artistic integrity in mind over what the individual player might be looking for in the packaging.

The box is designed to be printed and be folded together, so you can make physical copies. When fully printed out and folded, it should become a shoebox


First Ever website

This is the first website I made for my class, Web design 2. It was a basketball themed page which I take lots of pride in. Lots of things like the animations really make it feel like my own.


Snip of the website

The website is pretty simple, with a very simplistic color palatte, some small animations to fill negative space, and just a little bit of structure with divs and tables. But it was definetly one that I enjoyed making and helped me reinvigorate my passion for web design.
